- a TD(lambda) agent was implement based on the paper
- the paper collects 100 training sets, each with 10 trajectory and trains agent with various configs
- the training sets are collected through a random walk on a 7 state MarsRover env, with each trajectory starting at D
or in MarsRover equivalent state 3,
- this setup was reimplemented here and used to train the agent.

observation
- as in paper, the implemented agent was able to achieve similar minimum alpha values for give lambda as
reported in the report/report.md under week_3
- but the errors were scaled up somewhat.
- this might have something to do with how the training sets was generated, or so errors might be present in the
implementation of the td-lambda, which were not looked further due to time constraints.
